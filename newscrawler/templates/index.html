Some information about this project

This project was carried out as part of the Data_Engineering unit project.

It's a work in pairs with the objectives of retrieving data from a website, storing it and putting it forward on a site programmed with Flask.

Our choice fell on the SensCritique website and on the collection of Christmas films, in order to create a search engine dedicated to the various films on the Christmas theme.

It has been shown that Christmas movies are good for one's mood, and that in a period of covid, Christmas movies have been watched more than in other years: 20% of people started watching them in August, 37% during the month of September, and 80% of the people surveyed said they watched more Christmas movies in 2020 than ever.

We hope you enjoy this project and find your next favorite Christmas movie !
